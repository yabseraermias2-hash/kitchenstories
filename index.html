My Story

From my kitchen,
to yours.

I've been cooking since I could stand on a stool and reach the counter. What started as watching my grandmother peel tomatoes and fold dough has grown into a lifelong love affair with food — the smell of onions in butter, the weight of a well-seasoned cast iron, the way a dish can carry a whole memory in a single bite.

Kitchen Stories is where I share everything I make — from quick Tuesday pastas to the kind of Sunday roast that fills a house with warmth for hours. Every recipe has been cooked, tasted, adjusted, and cooked again. I hope you find something here that finds its way into your home, too.

Food has always been my love language — a way of saying I thought of you without saying a word. Pull up a chair.
